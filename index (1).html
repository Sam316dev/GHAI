Gilead Health-Bridge Africa Initiative is dedicated to improving health outcomes across underserved regions. We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental human right, not a privilege.

Governance & Transparency

We are committed to structural accountability and integrity in all our operations.

Our Constitution

Established to guide our programs and governance, the GHAI Constitution ensures equitable access to accurate health information across Africa through a transparent, democratic framework.

Our charter binds our General Congress and Executive Committee to strict operational accountability, requiring performance-based tenures and dual-signatory management of all organizational funds solely for community health interventions.

Constitution Highlights

Core Vision (Art. II)

To build a healthier Africa by bridging critical gaps in public health awareness, community access, and preventive medical care.

Key Action Areas (Art. IV)

  • Awareness: Targeting preventable conditions like hypertension, diabetes, and mental health disorders.
  • Outreach: Organizing community health workshops, clinics, and digital public health campaigns.
  • Education: Delivering health resources in clear, culturally relevant local languages.

Accountability (Art. IX)

GHAI maintains a strict corporate bank account requiring dual executive signatures. 100% of incoming donations and grants are direct-channeled into active field projects.
